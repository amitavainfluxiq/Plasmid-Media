How Solar Works

Solar panels absorb and convert sunlight (DC) electricity that flows to an inverter.

Inverters convert the direct current (DC) power into alternating (AC).

The AC electricity is then sent to the breaker box and harnessed to power your home or business.

Any excess energy created by your solar power system will flow into the utility grid through the meter.
